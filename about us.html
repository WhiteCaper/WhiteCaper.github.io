Cicis History at Fuqua

The company was founded in 1985 in Plano, Texas, and began franchising in 1987. In 2015, the company refreshed its branding, dropping the apostrophe to become “Cicis” and changing the logo. The area surrounding the Fuqua location transitioned from undeveloped land and farmhouses into a suburban landscape around the late 1960s and early 1970s. The development was heavily influenced by the construction of major infrastructure in the vicinity, including Beltway 8.
